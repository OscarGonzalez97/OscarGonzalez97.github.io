Side Projects

Here are a few of my side projects that I've proudly deployed into production:

  • Vetesoft: Vetesoft is software designed for veterinary management. The main module is intended for handling the veterinary clinic's patients and everything associated with them (weight records, appointments, treatments, analyses, vaccination plans, vaccinations, exams, or treatments). Users with the ability to edit all this information belong to the Veterinarian and/or Administrator group. The distinction between these roles is that the Administrator can create users of the Veterinarian and Client types and has access to a bit more information about the veterinary clinic in general.

  • Buscamascota: It is a platform designed to assist lost pets in reuniting with their owners, in a different and more efficient manner. Reports of lost, found, or sighted pets are generated through a form. The report information, along with the last known location of the animal marked on a map, is published. An image containing the report details can be downloaded and shared via WhatsApp or other means. The reports are stored and can be accessed through the search section, either by browsing the map view or using filters to refine the results.

  • Autogo: Autogo is a vehicle sales platform where users can upload all relevant information about their vehicles and connect with a seller.

  • Bootcamp landing: A landing page for my bootcamps, where my students and I collaborate on incredible projects.
